Logo Color

The main requirement for the color of the FDA-Go logo is to keep the font and accent consistent.

On a white background:

Text: #051869 - Dark navy blue

Arrow accent: #009f21 - Green

On a dark background:

Text: #ffffff - White

Arrow accent: #009f21 - Green
